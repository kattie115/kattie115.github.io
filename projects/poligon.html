The Challenge

While their website offers enough information, the design was a bit inclonventional. The redesign resulted in a site that reflects their uniqe approach in the creative fields.

The Solution - Redeign

While reviewing their website, I have noticed that the main thing that stood out to me was their photography (credit to David Lotric for them).

Inspired by the photos of the offices, work labs, and the community, I deceided to create a design that would showcase the photos without having to create a seperate gallery for them.

Since, the information they offer is straight and to the point, it only takes up half of the page to reduce the excesive amount of white space, while other half displays their photography.

Typography

For the primary typeface, I selceted Open Sans (light for the bigger titles, and regular for body text) for good readability. As a secondary typeface, I went with Cardo (italic for subtitles) which is a more classy font that compliments the blockness of Open Sans.
